**Specialty**: Comprehensive Non-QM and investment lending
**Programs**: Portfolio Select, Platinum, Bank Statement, ICF, CES/HELOC
**Strengths**: Multiple program options, competitive rates, high loan amounts
**Best For**: Investors with good credit seeking maximum flexibility

### InvestorX  
**Specialty**: DSCR-based investment property lending
**Strengths**: Property cash flow qualification, competitive rates
**Best For**: Investors with strong properties but complex personal income

### HomeXpress
**Specialty**: Non-QM programs including investor options
**Programs**: PrimeX, CoreX, InvestorXIncome, InvestorXDSCR, InvestorXNoRatio

**Best For**: Self-employed investors, alternative documentation scenarios

### LoanStream Wholesale
**Specialty**: Non-QM and DSCR investor programs
**Ranking**: #1 Non-QM Lender (2025), Top #4 Wholesale Lender
**Best For**: Brokers seeking Non-QM and investor solutions

### Archome LLC
**Specialty**: Non-QM, Jumbo, and Agency Plus programs  
**Strengths**: High loan amounts, competitive terms
**Best For**: High-net-worth investors

## Special Programs

### DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) Loans
**Available From**: AOMS ICF, HomeXpress InvestorXDSCR, InvestorX
**Qualification**: Based on property rental income vs. mortgage payment
**Minimum DSCR**: Typically 1.00 or higher
**Benefits**: No personal income documentation required

### Bank Statement Programs
**Available From**: AOMS Bank Statement, HomeXpress programs
**Documentation**: 12 or 24 months bank statements
**Best For**: Self-employed borrowers, non-traditional income

### No Ratio Programs  
**Available From**: HomeXpress InvestorXNoRatio
**Requirements**: 40% down payment, 640+ credit score
**Benefits**: No income or debt ratio calculations

### Foreign National Programs
**Available From**: AOMS ICF
**Special Requirements**: 
- 12 months reserves
- Assets in US account for 30 days
- Specific visa types accepted
- Not allowed in Osceola County, FL

## Key Considerations for Matching

1. **Credit Score Impact**: Higher credit scores unlock better rates and lower down payment options
2. **Down Payment Flexibility**: More down payment opens up more lender options
3. **Property Type**: Specialty properties (condotels, STR) limit lender options
4. **Documentation**: Self-employed borrowers need bank statement or DSCR programs
5. **Loan Amount**: Jumbo loans ($1M+) require specialized lenders
6. **Geographic Restrictions**: Some programs have state-specific limitations
7. **Investment Experience**: Some programs require prior investment property experience

## Usage Instructions for LLM

When a user provides their investment scenario:

1. **Extract Key Variables**:
   - Property value and type
   - Credit score
   - Down payment capability (%)
   - Loan amount needed
   - Employment/income type
   - Location

2. **Apply Filters**:
   - Start with down payment capability
   - Filter by credit score requirements  
   - Consider property type restrictions
   - Check loan amount limits

3. **Provide Recommendations**:
   - List 2-3 best matching lenders
   - Explain why each is suitable
   - Suggest alternatives if buyer can adjust terms
   - Highlight any special program benefits

4. **Include Caveats**:
   - Note that final approval depends on full underwriting
   - Mention that rates and terms can change
   - Suggest contacting lenders for current guidelines
